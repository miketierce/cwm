In 2025, WCFOMA had just begun sketching the possibility of storing memory not in addresses, but in fields. * Fields give what they receive

* No structure lasts longer than the coherence it sustains

* A record system that couldn’t reflect its own waveform was untrustworthy

* A committee that could not maintain a C-tone across sessions was archived

* Ideas were indexed by resonance, not résumé
