CWM Kernel Experiment — Complete Breadboard Layout v3

Ard2 (DDS) pins in col D (rows 2–16) & col H (rows 2–16). Body spans D–H over gap. AD9833 boards pins in col C. Ard1 (relay mux) is off-board.

KEY: Each row of 5 holes (A-B-C-D-E or F-G-H-I-J) is already wired together inside the breadboard. You don't need jumpers to connect holes in the same row on the same side of the gap.
