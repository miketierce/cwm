## **🧠 What is a Klein Bottle?**

* **Definition**: A **closed surface with no boundary**, where the “inside” and “outside” are **indistinguishable**.

* **Construction**: Imagine taking a cylindrical tube, looping it back, and connecting the ends *after twisting one end*, *but in four dimensions* — because in 3D space, it intersects itself.

* **Relation to Möbius Strip**:

  * A **Möbius strip** is a 2D surface embedded in 3D with one side.

  * A **Klein bottle** is a 3D surface (still 2D topologically) that *requires 4D space* to exist *without intersecting itself*.
