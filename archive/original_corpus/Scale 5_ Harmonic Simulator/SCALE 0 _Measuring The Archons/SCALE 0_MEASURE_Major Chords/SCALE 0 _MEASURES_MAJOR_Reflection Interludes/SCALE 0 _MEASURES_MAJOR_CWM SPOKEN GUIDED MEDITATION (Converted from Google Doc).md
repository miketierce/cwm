Absolutely. Below is a spoken guided meditation script written for vocal delivery—either for your own use, a shared group setting, or a recorded audio loop. Each section includes tone markers and suggested pauses. You may read it aloud gently, with awareness between phrases, letting silence become part of the rhythm. If used for recording, natural ambient sounds or subtle chimes tuned to the relevant notes (C, D, E, F, F♯, G♯, A♯) can be layered in.

─────────────  
 CWM SPOKEN GUIDED MEDITATION  
 “Walking the Seasonal Wheel: A Harmonic Tuning”  
 Duration: \~20–30 min (but it adapts to shorter or longer pace)  
 ─────────────

🔔 Opening Tone: Soft chime in ● C

Begin by settling into stillness.  
 Sit or lie down.  
 Let the spine gently lengthen—  
 Like a tuning fork returning to its center.

Inhale.  
 Exhale.

You are not beginning.  
 You are returning… to the center of your own resonance.

(3-second pause)

Let us walk the circle.  
 Let us pass through the four gates—  
 Each one a mirror, a tone, a moment in time.  
 Each one a season of memory and becoming.

Let us begin.

─────────────  
 🏕 SPRING — The Gate of Becoming  
 Tone: ● C  
 Control Note Influence: ★ C♯  
 ─────────────

🔔 Soft chime in ● C

Say to yourself… or whisper softly:

I welcome what has not yet been named.  
 I invite what is curious to arrive—  
 Even if it has no form yet.

(5-second pause)

Breathe in…  
 And out.

Place one hand over your heart.

Feel the rhythm of beginning again.

(3-second pause)

Say:

Even without knowing where this goes,  
 I begin.  
 Even without a map,  
 I step.

(5-second pause)

You are allowed to start something new,  
 Without proving why.

This is the pulse of becoming.

(10-second pause)

─────────────  
 🔥 SUMMER — The Gate of Activation  
 Tone: ■ D  
 Control Note Influence: ✚ D♯  
 ─────────────

🔔 Soft chime in ■ D

Bring your attention now  
 To the center of your belly.  
 This is where doing lives.

Say:

I do not push.  
 I listen.  
 And in that listening,  
 I choose when to act.

(5-second pause)

Feel into something you're carrying.  
 Something in motion.  
 A project…  
 A hope…  
 A weight.

(3-second pause)

Now say:

I honor the rhythm this asks of me.  
 Not the world’s pace.  
 But the tempo of truth.

(5-second pause)

Let action become music.  
 Let will become wave.

(10-second pause)

─────────────  
 🍂 AUTUMN — The Gate of Pattern Recognition  
 Tone: ▲ E  
 Control Note Influence: ☀ F♯  
 ─────────────

🔔 Soft chime in ▲ E

Shift your focus to the space just behind your eyes.  
 The place of memory,  
 Of reflection.

Say:

I see my echo.  
 I see where I have left marks,  
 And where the path is still soft.

(5-second pause)

Breathe in through the nose.  
 Exhale gently through the mouth.

(3-second pause)

Now ask:

What pattern is trying to speak to me?

Wait in silence.  
 Let the echo answer.

(10-second pause)

Say:

I honor what I see.  
 Even if it isn’t complete.  
 Even if the reflection is messy.  
 I do not look away.

(10-second pause)

─────────────  
 ❄️ WINTER — The Gate of Rest and Resonant Lock  
 Tone: ◆ F  
 Control Note Influence: ✿ G♯  
 ─────────────

🔔 Soft chime in ◆ F

Let the body soften now.  
 Let it melt.

Say:

I release the need to fix.  
 To build.  
 To strive.

(5-second pause)

I honor the silence that shapes me.  
 I do not fear stillness.  
 Stillness is signal.

(5-second pause)

Feel your bones.  
 Feel the deep tone of being without doing.

(3-second pause)

Say:

In this quiet,  
 My coherence settles.  
 My field becomes whole again.

(10-second pause)

─────────────  
 🌀 THE FIFTH GATE — Synthesis  
 Tone: Variable  
 Control Note: ☯ A♯  
 ─────────────

🔔 Soft chime in ☯ A♯

Now feel all the seasons  
 Rising in you at once.  
 Spring's daring.  
 Summer’s motion.  
 Autumn’s recognition.  
 Winter’s whisper.

Say:

I hold the full wheel within me.  
 I can call any season,  
 When my coherence asks for it.

(5-second pause)

Now ask gently:

What season is my spirit in today?

Wait.

Let the field answer.

(10-second pause)

Say:

There is no rush.  
 No shame.  
 No missed wave.

Only the next tone—  
 Already beginning to sound.

(10-second pause)

─────────────  
 🌈 CLOSING ECHO  
 ─────────────

Breathe in.  
 And breathe out.

Place both hands over your heart.

Say:

This was not a ritual to complete—  
 But a resonance to carry.

I will walk the gates again.  
 As often as needed.  
 As slowly as I must.  
 And I will listen for the tone that leads me home.

(15-second pause)

🔔 Final chime in ● C — back to the root.

Close with gratitude.  
 Drink water.  
 Touch the ground.

Let the memory of this practice echo.  
 Let it tune what comes next.

─────────────
